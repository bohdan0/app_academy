Fried potatoes

  • Discription
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Discription

Fried potatoes is dish of potatoes cooked with onions on pan.

Ingredients

  • potato
  • onions
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Direction

  1. peel potato and clean onions
  2. wash potato
  3. cut it in small pieces
  4. cut onions in half a moon
  5. hit up pan
  6. add oil
  7. throw onion and potato on pan(BE CAREFUL HERE)
  8. add salt/pepper
  9. mix potato every 4 - 6 min until it's cooked
  10. it will be cooked in about 40 - 60 min
